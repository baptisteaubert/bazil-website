## Image Specs for Facebook Ads

- **Recommended size:** 1080 x 1080px (square, 1:1 ratio)
- **Minimum:** 600 x 600px
- **File type:** JPG or PNG
- **Max file size:** 30MB
- **Text on image:** Keep under 20% of the image area (Meta reduces reach for text-heavy images)

## Important: Your Images Need Cropping

Your current images are rectangular (landscape/portrait). Facebook ads perform best with **square (1:1) crops**. When uploading to Ads Manager, Facebook will show a cropping tool — use it to center the most appealing part of each image.

For the strongest performance, consider pre-cropping your top 4 images to 1080x1080 before uploading.

## Optional: Add Logo Overlay

For brand recognition, consider adding the Bazil logo (logo-blanc.png — the white version) as a small watermark in one corner of each ad image. Keep it subtle — maybe 15% of the image width, in a corner with enough contrast.

Don't add text like "FREE" or "Try Now" on the image — that's what the ad copy and CTA button are for, and Meta penalises text-heavy images.
