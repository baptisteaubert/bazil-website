## Copy Guidelines for Future Variations

**What works for Bazil ads:**
- Lead with a relatable frustration (not knowing what to cook, wasting food, boring meals)
- Emphasise "personalised" — this isn't generic meal plans, it's built for YOU
- Mention specific diets by name (vegan, halal, keto, gluten-free) — people search for these
- Always mention FREE prominently — it removes the biggest objection
- The Instacart integration is a differentiator — highlight the end-to-end flow
- Keep paragraphs short (1–2 sentences each) for mobile readability

**What to avoid:**
- Don't say "AI-powered" or "algorithm" — people want results, not tech
- Don't list too many features — pick 2–3 per ad and go deep
- Don't use stock-photo language ("fuel your body", "unlock your potential") — be direct
- Don't mention price since it's free — instead frame it as "no sign-up, no paywall"
